Yoonest is the platform that lets you invest in Real Estate in the easiest way.

Quick introduction

I worked as a contractor for Yoonest from 2021 to 2022. During this time, I created 3 landing pages for both desktop and mobile devices. Additionally, I designed new features for the main product, specifically the comparison and geodecision features.

Along the way, I also assisted in setting up a UI Kit for the Product Lead, enabling him to be more autonomous in creating new features.

Geodecision

If you're uncertain about making the right investment choice, how can you determine the potential of a property or the activity level of its neighborhood?

That's where this feature comes in handy, simplifying your decision-making process and eliminating the need to open multiple tabs just to gather the necessary information.

Comparison feature

When faced with a wide array of options, comparing multiple items can be challenging, whether it's real estate properties or everyday groceries.

This feature empowers you to effortlessly compare several selected properties in one go, utilizing the most popular settings as a basis for evaluation.
