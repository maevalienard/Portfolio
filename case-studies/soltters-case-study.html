Explanations of Soltters

Soltters, an NFT project, was conceived and launched at the end of 2021, driven by a desire to learn more into this trending phenomenon.

A total of 999 otters have been generated randomly, each with a unique combination of premade background colors, skin colors, clothes, and accessories.

The project has fostered an engaging community through platforms like Twitter and Discord, amassing +500 members within a span of just 2 months.

The mint (launch) of the project surpassed our expectations, and we have established a collaboration with the renowned platform, MagicEden, as the host of our secondary market.

Landing Page

I took on the challenge of designing a minimalistic landing page, considering our limited engineering skills, with the aim of showcasing the project and providing a platform for minting.

To enhance user engagement, I strategically incorporated various otter combinations, adding an extra layer of incentive.
