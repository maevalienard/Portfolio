Quick introduction

An enduring lifelong passion has transformed into a temporary online shop, encompassing sales, and engaging social media posts. Through my passion for art, I have created 3 series of 4 to 5 illustrations, along with 5 exclusive pieces, each limited to a specific number of prints.

The most challenging aspect has been building a vibrant community around my art. Despite the hurdles, within just 3 months, I have amassed over 500 followers on social media.

Pop culture

I have created 5 illustrations inspired by beloved pop culture characters, including Pikachu, Snoopy, Kirby, the Powerpuff Girls, and Princess Bubblegum.

Japanese theme

Inspired by the captivating Japanese culture, I've crafted a collection of 5 illustrations that pay tribute to well-known artifacts. These pieces are a reflection to the world of anime and manga, which has been a constant source of inspiration for me.
