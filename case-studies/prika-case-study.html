Prika tracks all of your cryptocurrencies into one application.

Quick introduction

Cryptocurrencies are typically stored in various locations, such as wallets and blockchains, which can make it challenging to get a clear overview of your holdings and their performance. For example, if you have Ethereum in multiple wallets, determining the overall performance of your holdings can be a headache.

I assisted the Prika founders with the creation of a branding that would fit in the Blockchain ecosystem. Specifically, I provided them with a branding and a landing page, which they used to create the application afterwards.

Branding creation

The name Prika draws its inspiration from the spice 'Paprika,' hence the choice of an orange color as the primary hue. To complement it, a vibrant blue was selected as a versatile secondary color, suitable for various use cases such as shadow tones. The colors have been carefully applied to ensure AA color contrast for optimal readability of the text.

For a user-friendly experience, a combination of rounded fonts has been thoughtfully chosen for the project.

In order to accommodate the display of numerous logos and data within the application, a curated selection of icons has also been included in the UI Kit.
